Optional domain: budgettravelguide.org

Logo concept: A suitcase combined with a location pin and a dollar symbol.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide useful information and tools for travelers who want to explore destinations on a budget. The site will include:

  • Affordable travel destinations
  • Money-saving tips for transportation and accommodations
  • Local food recommendations
  • A simple trip planning guide
  • Interactive features using JavaScript

Scenarios

  • What are the most affordable countries to visit on a limited budget?
  • How can I save money on flights and accommodations?
  • What is a realistic daily budget for traveling in South America?

Color Schema

#2c3e50 (Dark Blue): Used for headers, navigation, and footer.

#27ae60 (Green): Used for buttons, links, and accent elements.

#f4f4f4 (Light Gray): Used as the main background color.

Typography

  • Arial, sans-serif: Used for body text and paragraphs.
  • Arial Bold: Used for headings and section titles.

These fonts were chosen for readability, simplicity, and compatibility across devices.
