Peru is one of the most biodiverse countries in the world and is located on the western coast of South America. It is home to over 80% of the world’s ecosystems, ranging from the Andes Mountains to the Amazon rainforest and the Pacific coastline. Peru is renowned for its ancient Inca heritage, including the iconic Machu Picchu, its rich culinary tradition—ranked among the best globally, and its vibrant indigenous cultures that continue to thrive today.
