Travel Explorer - Site Plan

Site Name

Travel Explorer

A website for discovering travel destinations and tips.

Site Purpose

The site helps users explore cities, find attractions, and plan trips easily.

Scenarios

  • What are the best places to visit in a city?
  • What food should I try when traveling?
